Privacy Policy

AI Chat to PDF Chrome Extension  ·  Last updated: January 2025

Short version: This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your data. Everything stays on your device.

1. What this extension does

AI Chat to PDF is a Chrome browser extension that reads the visible text content of AI chat conversations on supported platforms and converts them into a PDF document using your browser's built-in print functionality.

Supported platforms: ChatGPTClaudeGeminiPerplexity

2. Data we collect

None.

This extension does not collect, record, store, or transmit any personal data, conversation content, or usage information — to us or to any third party.

3. How the extension works

  • When you click the extension icon, it reads the text content of the current page directly in your browser.
  • That content is formatted and passed to a new browser window for printing.
  • The PDF is saved locally on your device via your browser's Save as PDF function.
  • No data is sent to any external server at any point.
  • No analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry of any kind are included.

4. Permissions explained

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab — to read the content of the currently open AI chat tab when you click the extension icon. It cannot access any other tabs.
  • scripting — to inject a small script that extracts message text from the page. This script runs only when you activate the extension.
  • Host permissions (chatgpt.com, claude.ai, gemini.google.com, perplexity.ai) — to limit extension access to only these specific domains. It cannot run on any other website.

5. Third-party services

This extension does not integrate with, send data to, or rely on any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms.

6. Children's privacy

This extension does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
